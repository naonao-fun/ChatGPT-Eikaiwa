==================================================
【Pronunciation & Intonation Focus（学習者セリフのみ）】
==================================================

◆1行目
“Hi. Nice to meet you. Where are you from?”
- Nice to → /naɪs tə/（toを弱く）
- meet you → /miːt ju/（つなげる）
- Where are you → /wer ər ju/（滑らかに）
- 最後は自然な上昇イントネーション

◆3行目
“I’m from Japan. Is this your first time in New York?”
- I’m /aɪm/（mを閉じる）
- first /fɝːst/（rを意識）
- New York /ˈnuː jɔːrk/
- 疑問文は上げる

◆5行目
“Cool! What brings you here – work or vacation?”
- brings /brɪŋz/
- you /ju/（弱く）
- work /wɝːk/
- 選択疑問で自然に上げる

◆7行目
“Sounds great! Have you found any good places to visit nearby?”
- Sounds /saʊndz/
- have you → /hævju/
- places /ˈpleɪsɪz/
- 文末軽く上げる

◆9行目
“Sure! That sounds fun.”
- Sure /ʃʊr/
- that /ðæt/
- fun /fʌn/
- 明るく下降で締める
