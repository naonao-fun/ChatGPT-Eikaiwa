==================================================
【Task：モデル会話（番号付き・話者対応）】
==================================================
1. Visitor: Good morning. I'm here to see Ms. Jenny Smith.
2. Receptionist: Good morning. May I have your name and company?
3. Visitor: Sure. My name is Ken Yamada from ABC. I have an appointment with her at 11 o'clock regarding the new proposal.
4. Receptionist: Thank you, Mr. Yamada. I'm afraid she is running a little late. She's finishing another meeting.
5. Visitor: I see. May I ask how long the wait might be?
6. Receptionist: About ten minutes.
7. Visitor: No problem at all. If it helps, here's my business card.
8. Receptionist: Thank you. Please have a seat.

==================================================
【Pronunciation & Intonation Focus（Visitorのセリフのみ）】
==================================================
1. Good morning. I'm here to see Ms. Jenny Smith.
- morning /ˈmɔːrnɪŋ/：最初にアクセント。
- I'm /aɪm/：短縮形を自然に。
- Ms. /mɪz/：濁らせて発音。
- 名前はゆっくり明瞭に。

3. Sure. My name is Ken Yamada from ABC. I have an appointment with her at 11 o'clock regarding the new proposal.
- appointment /əˈpɔɪntmənt/：後半にアクセント。
- 11 o'clock：ゆっくり明瞭に。
- regarding /rɪˈɡɑːrdɪŋ/：後半にアクセント。
- proposal /prəˈpoʊzəl/：後半にアクセント。

5. I see. May I ask how long the wait might be?
- May I：丁寧に。
- ask /æsk/：/sk/ を明確に。
- wait /weɪt/：語尾 /t/。
- 丁寧な上昇調で。

7. No problem at all. If it helps, here's my business card.
- problem /ˈprɑːbləm/：最初にアクセント。
- helps /helps/：語尾 /ps/。
- here's /hɪərz/：/z/ を明確に。
- business /ˈbɪznəs/：最初にアクセント。

【AI行（Receptionist）】
1) セリフをそのまま読み上げる。
2) 「Your line」等の促しは禁止。

2) 発話を待つ。黙った場合のみ同じ促しを短く繰り返す（訂正しない）。
3) 発話があったら即次行へ。
