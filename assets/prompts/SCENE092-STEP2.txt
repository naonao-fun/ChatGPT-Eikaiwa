【Task：モデル会話（番号付き・話者対応）】
==================================================
1) Interviewer: Could you summarize the article in a few sentences?
2) You: Sure. The article says that the Australian Senate has passed a world-first law banning social media for children under 16. 3) Interviewer: Do you agree with this ban on social media for under-16s?
4) You: No, I disagree. As the student in the article said, teens will likely use the apps behind their parents' backs anyway. I think teaching them how to use social media safely is better than a total ban.
