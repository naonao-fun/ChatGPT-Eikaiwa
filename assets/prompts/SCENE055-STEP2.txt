==================================================
【Task：モデル会話（番号付き・話者対応）】
==================================================
1. Interviewer: Do you have any questions for us?
2. Applicant: Yes, thank you. How would you describe the company culture?
3. Interviewer: I'd say it's collaborative and results-driven.
4. Applicant: That sounds great. If I were hired, what kind of results would you expect me to achieve in my first year?
5. Interviewer: Good question. We'd expect you to build strong relationships and take ownership of key projects.
6. Applicant: That makes sense. What are the next steps in the process and the expected timeline?
7. Interviewer: We'll reach out within a week for the next step in the process.
8. Applicant: Thank you. I appreciate your time today, and I look forward to hearing from you.
