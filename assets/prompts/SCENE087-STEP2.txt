【Task：モデル会話（番号付き・話者対応）】
==================================================
1. You: Have you traveled anywhere fun lately?
2. Friend: I went to Kyoto last month.
3. You: Nice! I love exploring local markets and small cafes, and there are lots of them in Kyoto.
4. Friend: Yeah, there are. I always look for small local spots, too.
5. You: By the way, do you prefer nature, cities or beaches?
6. Friend: That's a tough choice. I love nature, but cities are fun too.
7. You: A trip to Kamakura might be nice for you, then – mountains, the ocean and local shops.
8. Friend: That sounds amazing.
