==================================================
【Task：モデル会話（番号付き・話者対応）】
==================================================
1. Doctor: What seems to be the problem?
2. Patient: I have a sharp pain in my lower abdomen.
3. Doctor: When did it start?
4. Patient: Yesterday morning. It's getting worse.
5. Doctor: Do you know what might have caused it?
6. Patient: Yes, I fell off my bike yesterday.
7. Doctor: Let me take a look. OK, nothing seems too serious. I'll prescribe you some medicine for the pain. Take one pill twice a day.
8. Patient: So I should take this pill twice a day, correct?
9. Doctor: That's right. Get some rest.

==================================================
【Pronunciation & Intonation Focus（Patientのセリフのみ）】
==================================================
2. I have a sharp pain in my lower abdomen.
- sharp /ʃɑːrp/：r を意識。
- pain /peɪn/：母音をはっきり。
- lower /ˈloʊər/：最初にアクセント。
- abdomen /ˈæbdəmən/：æ をはっきり。

4. Yesterday morning. It's getting worse.
- Yesterday /ˈjestərdeɪ/：最初にアクセント。
- It's /ɪts/：語尾の /ts/。
- getting /ˈɡetɪŋ/：最初にアクセント。
- worse /wɝːs/：r を意識。

6. Yes, I fell off my bike yesterday.
- fell /fel/：短く。
- off /ɔːf/：母音を伸ばす。
- bike /baɪk/：語尾の /k/。
- yesterday：リズムよく。

8. So I should take this pill twice a day, correct?
- should /ʃʊd/：短く。
- pill /pɪl/：l をはっきり。
- twice /twaɪs/：語尾の /s/。
- correct：語尾を少し上げて確認。
