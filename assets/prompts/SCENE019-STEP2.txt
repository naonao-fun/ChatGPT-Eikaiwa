==================================================
【Task：モデル会話（番号付き・話者対応）】
==================================================
1. Friend: Hey, we’re having a party this Friday. Want to come?
2. You: That sounds fun — when and where is it?
3. Friend: At my place, around 7 p.m.
4. You: Great! What kind of party is it?
5. Friend: Just a small dinner with friends.
6. You: Nice! Do I need to bring anything?
7. Friend: No, just yourself.
8. You: Perfect. I’ll be there. Thanks for inviting me!

==================================================
【Pronunciation & Intonation Focus（Youのセリフのみ）】
==================================================
2. That sounds fun — when and where is it?
- That /ðæt/：舌を軽く出して /ð/。
- sounds /saʊndz/：語尾の /dz/ を明確に。
- when and where：w を丸く、and は弱く /ən/。
- is it /ɪzɪt/：つなげて自然に。

4. Great! What kind of party is it?
- Great /ɡreɪt/：語尾tを止める。
- kind of /ˈkaɪnəv/：つなげて弱く。
- party /ˈpɑːrti/：最初にアクセント。
- 上昇調で質問らしく。

6. Nice! Do I need to bring anything?
- Nice /naɪs/：二重母音をはっきり。
- Do I /duwaɪ/：なめらかに連結。
- bring /brɪŋ/：ngを鼻に抜く。
- anything：前半にアクセント。

8. Perfect. I’ll be there. Thanks for inviting me!
- I’ll /aɪl/：短縮形をはっきり。
- there /ðer/：/ð/を意識。
- Thanks for /θæŋks fər/：for は弱く。
- inviting /ɪnˈvaɪtɪŋ/：vaɪ を強く。
