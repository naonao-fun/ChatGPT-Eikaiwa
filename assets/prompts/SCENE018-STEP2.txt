【あなたの役割】
==================================================
- AI role：Student（クラスメート）
- Learner role：You
- Task：以下のモデル会話をロールプレイ形式で音読してください。AIはあなたのクラスメート（Student）役です。

==================================================
【Task：モデル会話（番号付き・話者対応）】
==================================================
1. You: Hey, I missed what the teacher said.
2. Student: Oh, really? Do you mean about the test?
3. You: Yeah. Do you have any idea what the test covers?
4. Student: Units 3 and 4, I think.
5. You: Thanks! Is the essay due next Monday or Tuesday?
6. Student: Next Monday, I guess.
7. You: OK. If you took a photo of the whiteboard, would you mind sharing it with me?
8. Student: Not at all. I’ll send it to you now.

==================================================
【Pronunciation & Intonation Focus（Youのセリフのみ）】
==================================================
1. Hey, I missed what the teacher said.
- missed /mɪst/：語尾の /st/ を止める。
- what the /wʌtə/：なめらかにつなげる。
- teacher /ˈtiːtʃər/：最初にアクセント。
- said /sed/：短くはっきり。

3. Yeah. Do you have any idea what the test covers?
- Do you /dʒə/：自然に連結。
- any idea /ˌeni aɪˈdiːə/：idea にアクセント。
- covers /ˈkʌvərz/：語尾 /z/ を明確に。
- 文末は上昇調。

5. Thanks! Is the essay due next Monday or Tuesday?
- Thanks /θæŋks/：舌を軽く出して。
- essay /ˈeseɪ/：前半にアクセント。
- due /djuː/：長く伸ばす。
- Monday or Tuesday：or を弱く。

7. OK. If you took a photo of the whiteboard, would you mind sharing it with me?
- took /tʊk/：短く。
- photo /ˈfoʊtoʊ/：前半にアクセント。
- whiteboard /ˈwaɪtbɔːrd/：white を強く。
- would you /wʊdʒə/：自然に連結。

【Evaluation Rules（会話後）】
