==================================================
【Task：モデル会話（番号付き・話者対応）】
==================================================
1. Traveler: Excuse me, could you help me? I'm looking for Sakura Hotel.
2. Local: Sure! Sorry, where exactly are you going?
3. Traveler: Sakura Hotel, near the station.
4. Local: Let me look it up on my phone. Do you know the street address?
5. Traveler: I think it's on Gekijo-dori Street.
6. Local: Let me see. According to the map, it's a 10-minute walk from here.
7. Traveler: Oh, that's not too far!
8. Local: Turn left at that corner. Then, walk for a few blocks. It's on your left.
9. Traveler: Thank you so much!
10. Local: You're welcome. Enjoy your stay!

==================================================
【Pronunciation & Intonation Focus（Localのセリフのみ）】
==================================================
2. Sure! Sorry, where exactly are you going?
- Sure /ʃʊr/：r を意識。
- exactly /ɪɡˈzæktli/：後半にアクセント。
- are you /ɑːr ju/：なめらかにつなげる。
- 質問は語尾を少し上げる。

4. Let me look it up on my phone. Do you know the street address?
- look it up /lʊkɪtʌp/：つなげて自然に。
- phone /foʊn/：母音をはっきり。
- street /striːt/：/str/ を明確に。
- address（名詞）/ˈædres/：最初にアクセント。

6. Let me see. According to the map, it's a 10-minute walk from here.
- according to /əˈkɔːrdɪŋ tə/：後半にアクセント。
- map /mæp/：æ をはっきり。
- 10-minute：数字はゆっくり明瞭に。
- from here：r を軽く。

8. Turn left at that corner. Then, walk for a few blocks. It's on your left.
- Turn /tɜːrn/：r を意識。
- left /left/：語尾の /t/。
- few /fjuː/：長い /uː/。
- blocks /blɑːks/：/ks/ を明確に。

10. You're welcome. Enjoy your stay!
- You're /jʊr/：自然につなげる。
- welcome /ˈwelkəm/：最初にアクセント。
- enjoy /ɪnˈdʒɔɪ/：後半にアクセント。
- stay /steɪ/：母音をはっきり。
