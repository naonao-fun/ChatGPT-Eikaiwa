==================================================
【Task：モデル会話（番号付き・話者対応）】
==================================================
1. Visitor: Excuse me, could you tell me where the Main Hall is?
2. Local: Of course. If you follow this main path, you'll reach the Main Hall in about three minutes.
3. Visitor: OK. Is there an entrance fee?
4. Local: Yes. The entrance fee is 300 yen per person.
5. Visitor: Great. Is there anything else to see?
6. Local: The bell tower is here on the map. The bell-ringing is at 4 p.m. if you'd like to watch.
7. Visitor: Perfect, I'll check it out. Thanks!
8. Local: You're welcome. Enjoy your visit!
