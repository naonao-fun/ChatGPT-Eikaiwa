==================================================
【Task：モデル会話（番号付き・話者対応）】
==================================================
1) Host: Hi! Welcome to our guesthouse.
2) Guest: Hi, I’ve booked Room 202.
3) Host: Great. Your room uses a passcode instead of a key.
4) Guest: I see. Could you show me how to unlock the door?
5) Host: Sure. Just enter this four-digit code — 4932 — on the keypad.
6) Guest: Got it. What time should I check out tomorrow?
7) Host: By 11 a.m., please.
8) Guest: Thanks. What’s the Wi-Fi password, by the way?
9) Host: It’s written on the card next to the TV.
10) Guest: Perfect. Thank you.

==================================================
【Pronunciation & Intonation Focus（学習者セリフのみ）】
==================================================
2) Hi, I’ve booked Room 202.
- I’ve /aɪv/（「アイヴ」ではなく一息で）
- booked /bʊkt/（語尾 /kt/ をはっきり）
- Room 202（two-oh-two と明瞭に）
- 文末は軽く下げる

4) I see. Could you show me how to unlock the door?
- Could you → /kʊdʒə/（弱形）
- show /ʃoʊ/（「ショー」伸ばしすぎない）
- unlock /ʌnˈlɑːk/（アクセントは lock）
- 疑問文は最後をやや上げる

6) Got it. What time should I check out tomorrow?
- Got it /ɡɑːtɪt/（tを軽くつなぐ）
- should I → /ʃʊdaɪ/（弱形）
- check out（/tʃek aʊt/ つなげる）
- tomorrow は後半にアクセント

8) Thanks. What’s the Wi-Fi password, by the way?
- What’s /wʌts/（tsを明瞭に）
- Wi-Fi /ˈwaɪfaɪ/（ワイファイ）
- password /ˈpæsˌwɝːd/（前半強く）
- by the way は軽く区切る

10) Perfect. Thank you.
- Perfect /ˈpɝːfɪkt/（最初にアクセント）
- Thank you → /θæŋkjuː/（thをはっきり）
- 落ち着いて下げ調子で締める
