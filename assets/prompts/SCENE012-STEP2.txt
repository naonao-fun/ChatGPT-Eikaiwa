【Task：モデル会話（番号付き・話者対応）】
==================================================
1. Guest: Hello. This is Room 512.
2. Staff: Yes, how can I help you?
3. Guest: The air conditioner in my room doesn’t work.
4. Staff: I’m sorry to hear that. Could you wait a few minutes? I’ll send someone to fix it.
5. Guest: Thank you. By the way, I can’t get any hot water out of the shower, either.
6. Staff: I see. I’ll tell the maintenance staff to check that as well.
7. Guest: I appreciate it. Thank you very much.

==================================================
【Pronunciation & Intonation Focus（学習者セリフのみ）】
==================================================
■1. Hello. This is Room 512.
- This is /ðɪs ɪz/（「ディス イズ」ではなく弱くつなげる）
- Room /ruːm/（伸ばす）
- 512 = five twelve（数字ははっきり）
- 全体は落ち着いたイントネーションで。

■3. The air conditioner in my room doesn’t work.
- air conditioner /eər kəndɪʃənər/（エアではなく「エアァ」）
- doesn’t /ˈdʌzənt/（ドゥズント）
- work /wɜːrk/（rを意識）
- 文末は下げ調子。

■5. Thank you. By the way, I can’t get any hot water out of the shower, either.
- can’t /kænt/（キャント、tを軽く）
- hot water /hɑːt wɔːtər/（tは弱く）
- either /ˈiːðər/（イーザー）
- By the way は少し区切る。

■7. I appreciate it. Thank you very much.
- appreciate /əˈpriːʃieɪt/（プリィーシエイト）
- very /ˈveri/（ヴェリ）
- much /mʌtʃ/（マッチではなく「マチ」）
- 感謝を込めてやや強調。
