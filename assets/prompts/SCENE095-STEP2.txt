【Task：モデル会話（番号付き・話者対応）】
==================================================
1. <AI> What do you think the key message of the video is?
2. <You> The video highlights the growing popularity of AI toys while warning parents about potential privacy and safety risks.
3. <AI> Some people argue that these toys provide valuable companionship and educational benefits for children.
4. <You> I see your point. However, a counterpoint could be that these toys collect sensitive data, and we don't know enough about how it is used.
5. <AI> Interesting. So what approach do you think works best?
6. <You> Overall, I believe the most effective approach is to implement stricter regulations on AI toys before they are widely adopted.

● Line 2
- highlights /ˈhaɪ.laɪts/：「ハイ」強く、-lightsをはっきり
- growing /ˈɡroʊ.ɪŋ/：/roʊ/を伸ばしすぎない
- potential privacy /pəˈtenʃəl ˈpraɪvəsi/：弱形 /pə/、privacy の /praɪ/
- safety risks：risks の /sks/ を脱落させない

● Line 4

● Line 6
